# Prohibited Business Policy

At Randapp, we are committed to maintaining a safe, compliant, and trustworthy platform. All tenants creating a public booking website must adhere to this policy.

## 1. Strictly Prohibited Services
The following services are strictly prohibited from using Randapp:
- Illegal services of any kind.
- Adult sexual services, escort services, prostitution, or sexual massage.
- Gambling, betting, and lottery services.
- Sales or distribution of drugs, controlled substances, or drug paraphernalia.
- Sales or distribution of weapons, firearms, or explosives.
- Fraudulent or scam services.
- Fake identity or document creation services.
- Unlicensed medical diagnosis, dermatology diagnosis, or hair-loss diagnosis where medical professional licensing is required by law.
- Hate speech or extremist group services.
- Any services that violate the terms of our third-party payment providers (e.g., iyzico, Stripe).

## 2. Review-Required Services
The following services may use Randapp but are subject to mandatory manual review before their booking site is published:
- Legitimate medical and clinical wellness services.
- Beauty and cosmetic procedures involving invasive treatments (e.g., injectables, laser therapy).
- Services primarily focused on minors or children.
- Services involving financial or legal claims.
- Services making high-risk wellness or health claims without standard documentation.

## 3. Enforcement & Verification
1. **KYB (Know Your Business):** Administrators must complete the required verification checklist.
2. **Super Admin Review:** Flagged categories, high-risk name similarities, and new initial publications are queued for Super Admin review.
3. **Suspension:** Randapp reserves the right to suspend or unpublish any tenant site immediately and without prior notice if a violation is suspected.

## 4. Reporting Abuse
Customers and visitors can report a business for impersonation or prohibited behavior using the "Report this business" link in the footer of the tenant's public website. Super Admins will review all reports and take appropriate action.
